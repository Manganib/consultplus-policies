Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 November 2025

ConsultPlus (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a digital health application that provides guided medical history collection, AI-assisted symptom assessment, chronic care follow-up tools, and optional consultation with licensed medical practitioners in Zambia.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal health data is processed safely, securely, and transparently.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Personal information: name, phone number, email
  • Demographic data: age, sex
  • Medical history & symptoms
  • Medication lists
  • Pregnancy-related information
  • Chronic condition information
  • Images you upload (e.g., wounds, rashes, lab results)
  • Voice notes (paid tier)

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device model and OS
  • IP address
  • Usage analytics
  • Firebase Authentication identifiers
  • Crash and performance logs

C. Sensitive Health Information

Because this is a health app, we may collect:

  • Medical conditions
  • Treatment history
  • Vital symptoms
  • Laboratory results
  • Pregnancy status
  • Mental health information

We treat this information with enhanced protection.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide AI-assisted symptom guidance
  • Improve accuracy of health suggestions
  • Enable communication with healthcare professionals
  • Support chronic care management
  • Customize medical content to your profile
  • Send reminders for medications or follow-ups
  • Comply with legal requirements

We do not sell your data.

3. Use of AI

ConsultPlus uses AI to:

  • Assist with symptom triage
  • Provide educational information
  • Generate question prompts
  • Summarize your medical history for a clinician

AI suggestions do not replace a medical professional’s judgment.

All final clinical decisions must be made by a licensed practitioner.

4. Data Sharing

A. With Licensed Clinicians

If you opt for a consultation, clinicians may access:

  • Your medical history
  • Uploaded images
  • AI-generated summaries

B. With Service Providers

  • Firebase (data storage)
  • Google Cloud (secure servers)
  • Payment processors (for subscriptions)

C. When Required by Law

To comply with Zambian law or court orders.

We never share your data for marketing without your consent.

5. Data Retention

  • Medical records: 7 years
  • Images: until deleted by user
  • AI conversation logs: 12 months
