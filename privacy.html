Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 November 2025

ConsultPlus (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a digital health application that provides guided medical history collection, AI-assisted symptom assessment, chronic care follow-up tools, and optional consultation with licensed medical practitioners in Zambia. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal health data is processed safely, securely, and transparently. 1. Information We Collect A. Information You Provide Personal information: name, phone number, email Demographic data: age, sex Medical history & symptoms Medication lists Pregnancy-related information Chronic condition information Images you upload (e.g., wounds, rashes, lab results) Voice notes (paid tier) B. Automatically Collected Information Device model and OS IP address Usage analytics Firebase Authentication identifiers Crash and performance logs C. Sensitive Health Information Because this is a health app, we may collect: Medical conditions Treatment history Vital symptoms Laboratory results Pregnancy status Mental health information We treat this information with enhanced protection. 2. How We Use Your Information We use your data to: Provide AI-assisted symptom guidance Improve accuracy of health suggestions Enable communication with healthcare professionals Support chronic care management Customize medical content to your profile Send reminders for medications or follow-ups Comply with legal requirements We do not sell your data. 3. Use of AI ConsultPlus uses AI to: Assist with symptom triage Provide educational information Generate question prompts Summarize your medical history for a clinician AI suggestions do not replace a medical professional’s judgment. All final clinical decisions must be made by a licensed practitioner. 4. Data Sharing We may share your data: A. With Licensed Clinicians If you opt for a consultation, clinicians may access: Your medical history Uploaded images AI-generated summaries B. With Service Providers Firebase (data storage) Google Cloud (secure servers) Payment processors (for subscriptions) C. When Required by Law In compliance with Zambian law or court orders. We never share your data for marketing without your consent. 5. Data Retention Medical records: 7 years (standard medical retention) Images: until deleted by user AI conversation logs: 12 months Account data: until account deletion Users may request data deletion at any time. 6. Security We follow industry-standard security practices: Encrypted data transmission Secure cloud storage Role-based access control Regular security audits No system is 100% secure, but we take all reasonable steps to protect your information. 7. Your Rights You may: Request access to your data Request corrections Request deletion Withdraw consent Export your data Contact: support@consultplus.health 8. Children’s Privacy ConsultPlus is not intended for users under 16 without parental consent. 9. International Data Transfers Your data may be stored in: Zambia Europe United States (depending on Firebase servers) All transfers comply with international data protection standards. 10. Contact For questions: support@consultplus.health
